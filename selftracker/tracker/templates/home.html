Self Tracking

What happens when people turn their everyday experience into data. In this application, a user can record data in a tabular form. The registration is done immediately. Therefore, self-tracking of data can be a valuable source of information today, as smartphones, smartwatches and other so-called wearables enable a quick and easy data recording.

Application is compatible for Web page, Android and IOS devices.

Self Tracking Application is still in development phase.
